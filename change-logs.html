Release notes

What changed across selected GoEatery versions

Each entry is written for GoEatery and trimmed to the most useful product-facing changes.

v1.3.08 Jun 16, 2026

Reports, KOT controls, and permissions polish

  • Added an order-wise detailed report for deeper sales review.
  • Added a canceled KOT print option for kitchen and audit workflows.
  • Improved table locking to prevent duplicate orders on the same table.
  • Fixed due-payment totals, loyalty behavior, kiosk sessions, customer icons, and POS interface issues.
  • Separated staff permissions for billing, payment, and item deletion actions.
v1.3.02 May 24, 2026

QR, ordering, reservations, and dashboard refresh

  • Redesigned QR code management with preview, regenerate, download, and print actions.
  • Improved customer ordering so dine-in, delivery, pickup, and reservation flows feel clearer.
  • Enhanced reservation management with area support, better assignment, and updated booking communication.
  • Refined dashboard, menu copying, image handling, branding, app download settings, delivery details, and POS printing.
  • Improved admin permissions, onboarding, support tickets, contact pages, profiles, and general platform stability.
v1.3.0 May 13, 2026

Menu compliance, POS catalog, and order completion settings

  • Added allergen and dietary label support for menu items.
  • Added a setting to automatically mark paid orders as completed.
  • Updated the POS menu item catalog and fixed related POS issues.
  • Restricted waiters so they can only select themselves where required.
v1.2.97 May 09, 2026

Menu upload, POS fixes, and offline payment support

  • Fixed menu image upload problems reported by some accounts.
  • Improved add-customer behavior from the POS during new KOT creation.
  • Fixed bulk menu uploads, order deletion, and POS design issues.
  • Added support for recording offline payments.
v1.2.9 May 05, 2026

Major POS refresh and offline ordering foundation

  • Revamped the POS experience across the checkout workspace.
  • Introduced early offline POS capability.
  • Added OpenStreetMap support.
  • Shipped additional bug fixes around the updated POS experience.
v1.2.75 Mar 25, 2026

Waiter, delivery assignment, and mobile fixes

  • Adjusted waiter status handling so new waiter actions start pending.
  • Improved action-button loaders across the platform.
  • Limited delivery assignment so one order is assigned to one delivery person at a time.
  • Fixed table clicks, POS reload delays, mobile sidebar behavior, customer-site language issues, and multiple KOT problems.
v1.2.73 Mar 20, 2026

Delivery partner workspace and reporting improvements

  • Added admin and partner-facing delivery sections.
  • Improved COD tracking at the order level.
  • Added waiter and chef notifications.
  • Improved sales reporting and shipped general fixes.
v1.2.72 Mar 13, 2026

Restaurant status, tax import, and delivery access

  • Added restaurant open and close settings.
  • Added branch tax import support.
  • Added refund amount button permissions.
  • Introduced delivery executive login and control panel updates.
v1.2.71 Mar 06, 2026

Delivery partners, maps, and waiter confirmation

  • Added a delivery partner section.
  • Added restaurant open and close controls.
  • Added waiter order confirmation.
  • Added Google Maps support on restaurant pages and blocked discounts after billing.
v1.2.62 Jan 23, 2026

Tax, permissions, refunds, and order handling

  • Fixed tax calculations across POS and reports.
  • Improved permissions, customer orders, table requirements, mobile trial UI, realtime updates, and dark-mode text.
  • Added support-ticket visibility controls, KOT creation for existing orders, time format settings, package descriptions, and report filters.
  • Added pickup customer details, Mollie payments, waiter-table linking, refunds, menu-by-table QR choices, and order merging.
v1.2.60 Dec 11, 2025

Draft orders, table merging, and POS speed

  • Added table merge support for unpaid orders.
  • Added draft orders and customer-facing Wi-Fi credential display.
  • Optimized POS interface speed.
  • Fixed translation, dark-mode FAQ visibility, customer login, and phone country-code issues.
v1.2.55 Nov 12, 2025

Pickup details, receipts, and package limits

  • Added settings to disable the POS order-type popup and customer-site header.
  • Captured customer name and phone for pickup orders.
  • Added table-change confirmation for existing orders.
  • Added customer phone visibility on receipts plus paid/unpaid receipt indicators.
  • Added package limits for orders and menu items.
v1.2.53 Oct 23, 2025

Delivery app reporting and token fixes

  • Added delivery app reporting.
  • Fixed order token number behavior.
v1.2.52 Oct 23, 2025

Order-type pricing and delivery app settings

  • Added separate pricing by order type.
  • Added delivery app settings under order settings.
  • Fixed Paddle subscription payment behavior.
  • Added a terms-and-conditions link option for signup forms.
v1.2.48 Oct 14, 2025

Token screens, registration consent, and reporting fixes

  • Added token number management and customer screen token display.
  • Added registration options for terms and privacy acceptance.
  • Allowed waiter assignment after billing and added a markdown editor for landing pages.
  • Fixed sales reports, modifiers, customer order auto-printing, PayPal, table QR behavior, and customer display issues.
  • Prepared compatibility for the cash register module.
v1.2.44 Sep 26, 2025

Transfers, KOT status visibility, and translation fixes

  • Fixed Xendit gateway behavior and contact page background images.
  • Added bank transfer records to reports.
  • Fixed translation, delivery executive, and order number issues.
  • Showed KOT status on customer orders and added an undo option for KOT status.
v1.2.43 Sep 08, 2025

Menu images, staff security, and printer language support

  • Added menu image visibility controls, Xendit payments, superadmin creation, and staff password changes.
  • Fixed order issues, kitchen permissions, languages, and auto refresh.
  • Improved thermal printer support for more languages and symbols.
  • Added bulk menu item upload.
v1.2.42 Aug 14, 2025

Customer display, reservations, and delivery fees

  • Added customer display as a package feature.
  • Added delete-order confirmation and bank transfer in payment entry.
  • Improved currency-aware pricing plan display.
  • Added reservation restrictions, guest minimums, and POS delivery fee support.
v1.2.38 Jul 22, 2025

Customer display, tax setup, and staff roles

  • Added restaurant login details to welcome emails.
  • Added a customer display screen.
  • Added item-level tax configuration and predefined payment amount settings.
  • Added custom staff role creation and fixed interface and workflow bugs.
v1.2.36 Jul 09, 2025

Direct printing, realtime KOT, and waiter reporting

  • Added beta direct printing through printer settings.
  • Added realtime updates for orders and KOT activity.
  • Added waiter filtering to sales reports.
  • Fixed customer logout routing and payment gateway visibility issues.
v1.2.35 Jun 27, 2025

Branch import and POS action fixes

  • Added branch creation support from existing branch data.
  • Fixed mobile footer/header menu placement on landing pages.
  • Fixed split payment by quantity and KOT deletion cases.
  • Added a combined POS action for KOT, bill, and payment.
  • Fixed untranslated email content.
v1.2.33 Jun 20, 2025

Payment gateways, item notes, sorting, and secure order pages

  • Added item notes, menu sorting, per-item KOT ready status, and KOT settings.
  • Added Paystack, PayPal, and Payfast payment options with admin-level gateway controls.
  • Added footer links, signup phone field, and direct print without popup.
  • Fixed split quantities, past reservation times, waiter selection, payment amount edits, delivery fees, and order success page security.
v1.2.32 Jun 04, 2025

Live reload, delivery fees, and customer assignment fixes

  • Added order live-reload enable and disable control.
  • Fixed delivery fee visibility on bills.
  • Fixed customer info editing, existing-order assignment, paid-order payment changes, and duplicated customer details.
v1.2.30 May 20, 2025

Delivery settings, landing customization, and reservation fixes

  • Added delivery settings with multiple admin controls.
  • Added landing page and image customization for platform admins.
  • Fixed call-waiter permissions, reservations, restaurant charges, order cancellation, record deletion, and other issues.
v1.2.29 Apr 23, 2025

Flutterwave, booking details, and safer paid orders

  • Added Flutterwave payment gateway support.
  • Added name and phone fields to table booking.
  • Added waiter filtering in orders.
  • Prevented paid orders from being deleted, while still allowing cancellation.
v1.2.20 Mar 21, 2025

PWA, package, and KOT print fixes

  • Fixed PWA-related issues.
  • Removed an incorrectly included import report feature from packages.
  • Fixed KOT print details and other general issues.
v1.2.19 Mar 17, 2025

Inventory add-on, customer creation, and SEO fields

  • Released the inventory module add-on.
  • Added customer creation from the product flow.
  • Added SEO title control for landing pages.
  • Made package add-on features visible on pricing plans.
v1.2.18 Mar 07, 2025

Menu translations and charge controls

  • Added multilingual support for menu items.
  • Added enable and disable controls for extra restaurant charges.
  • Fixed split-bill service charge behavior and favicon edge cases.
v1.2.14 Feb 24, 2025

Branding, QR receipt settings, and call-waiter setup

  • Added favicon upload in theme settings.
  • Added QR code controls in receipt settings.

  • Shipped general bug fixes.
