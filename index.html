FAQs

A few common questions from restaurant owners.

What is GoEatery?

GoEatery is a restaurant and bar management platform for sales, orders, reservations, menus, customer ordering pages, and day-to-day operations.

Is GoEatery mobile-friendly?

Yes. It is responsive and supports progressive web app usage across mobile and desktop devices.

Can I manage staff permissions?

Yes. Each staff member can have a separate login with role-based access and permissions.
