Questions restaurant owners ask before moving to GoEatery

Short answers for teams comparing POS, ordering, and restaurant management platforms.

What is GoEatery?

GoEatery is a cloud restaurant and bar management platform for sales, POS, reservations, menus, online ordering, kitchen tickets, customer records, payments, and reports.

Can my staff use different permissions?

Yes. Owners can create separate staff logins and assign role-based access for cashiers, managers, kitchen teams, and admins.

Does GoEatery support online ordering and QR menus?

Yes. You can create customer-facing ordering pages for QR menus, delivery, pickup, and direct online orders.

Can I manage more than one branch?

Yes. Supported plans include multi-location controls for menus, staff, orders, and reporting.

How long does setup usually take?

Most teams can get started the same day with menu setup, branch creation, staff roles, and basic printer or ticket settings.

Does GoEatery handle payments and receipts?

Yes. You can record cash and card payments, track payment status, and print or share receipts from the checkout flow.

Can I track stock and menu availability?

Yes. Inventory tools help you monitor items, hide unavailable products, and keep menus aligned with what is actually in stock.

What kind of support is available?

Our team can help with onboarding, configuration, branch setup, and day-to-day support through the usual support channels.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can move to a different plan as your team grows or your operational needs change.

Does the system work for delivery and takeout?

Yes. GoEatery supports dine-in, takeaway, pickup, and delivery workflows from the same checkout flow.
